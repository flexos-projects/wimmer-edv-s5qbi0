### Website Feature Inventory & Prioritization

This inventory outlines the specific features required to realize the project vision, prioritized for a phased rollout. The focus is on delivering maximum impact at launch (P1), followed by enhancements that build authority and add value.

---

#### **P1: Launch Essentials (Must-Have for Go-Live)**

These features are non-negotiable and form the core of the new trust-building engine.

*   **Homepage Hero Section:**
    *   **Description:** The first impression. Must feature a compelling headline, a sub-headline communicating the core value proposition (local, certified, trusted), a primary call-to-action (CTA), and visually integrated trust seals (Microsoft/Veeam Gold logos, 5-star rating).
    *   **Goal:** Instantly establish credibility and guide the user's next step.
*   **Human-Centric "About Us" Page:**
    *   **Description:** A dedicated page featuring a professional photo and biography of the founder, Thomas Wimmer. It will tell the story of his 10+ years of experience and the company's philosophy.
    *   **Goal:** Humanize the brand and connect expertise to an accountable person.
*   **Benefit-Driven Service Pages:**
    *   **Description:** A parent "Services" page with cards for each of the four pillars, linking to dedicated detail pages. Each detail page will explain the service, focus on client benefits, list key features, and have a relevant CTA.
    *   **Goal:** Clearly articulate what they do and why it matters to a business owner.
*   **Dynamic Trust Signal Showcase:**
    *   **Description:**
        *   **Certification Logos:** Prominently display Microsoft, Veeam, and Sophos partner logos in the site-wide footer and on relevant pages.
        *   **Testimonial Integration:** Sprinkle high-impact quotes from real clients throughout the homepage, service pages, and contact page.
        *   **Dedicated "Client Success" Page:** A central page to house all long-form testimonials and future case studies.
    *   **Goal:** Integrate social proof on every step of the user journey.
*   **High-Conversion Contact Page:**
    *   **Description:** A clean, simple page with all essential contact info (address, phone, email), an embedded Google Map, and a straightforward contact form with minimal fields (Name, Company, Email, Message).
    *   **Goal:** Make it effortless for a potential client to get in touch.
*   **Sticky Header with Click-to-Call:**
    *   **Description:** A site-wide header that remains visible on scroll, containing the main navigation, logo, and a highly visible phone number that is tappable on mobile devices.
    *   **Goal:** Ensure contact information and navigation are always accessible.
*   **Fully Responsive (Mobile-First) Design:**
    *   **Description:** The entire site must be flawlessly designed and functional on all devices, from large desktops to small smartphones.
    *   **Goal:** Provide a professional experience for all users, especially those finding the business on-the-go.

---

#### **P2: Post-Launch (First 3 Months)**

These features will be developed after the core site is live to build on the new foundation.

*   **Simple Blog/News Section ("Expertise Hub"):**
    *   **Description:** A basic blog system to publish articles offering IT tips, security warnings, and company news.
    *   **Goal:** Demonstrate ongoing expertise, improve SEO with fresh content, and create shareable assets.
*   **Develop First Case Study:**
    *   **Description:** Work with a top client to create one detailed case study (Problem, Solution, Result) with photos and a strong testimonial. This will serve as a template for future ones.
    *   **Goal:** Provide powerful, in-depth proof of their problem-solving capabilities.
*   **Automated Google Review Feed:**
    *   **Description:** Implement a widget that automatically pulls in and displays the latest 5-star Google Reviews on the website.
    *   **Goal:** Keep social proof fresh and automate the process of showcasing positive feedback.

---

#### **P3: Future Enhancements (6-12 Months)**

Long-term goals to expand the site's utility.

*   **Resource Center / Knowledge Base:**
    *   **Description:** Expand the blog into a more structured resource center with downloadable guides (e.g., "Cybersecurity Checklist for SMEs") in exchange for an email address.
    *   **Goal:** Lead generation and further establishment as a thought leader.
*   **Online Appointment Booking Integration:**
    *   **Description:** Integrate a system like Calendly to allow potential clients to book an initial 15-minute consultation directly from the website.
    *   **Goal:** Reduce friction in the sales process.
