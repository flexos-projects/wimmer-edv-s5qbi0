### **Page Details & Content Strategy**

**1. Home (`/`)**
*   **Purpose:** To build maximum trust in minimum time and direct users to the most relevant next step.
*   **SEO Title:** `Wimmer EDV | Ihr Gold-Zertifizierter IT-Partner in St. Pölten`
*   **Meta Description:** `Zuverlässige IT-Betreuung für KMU in St. Pölten. Microsoft Gold Partner, 5-Sterne-Bewertungen. Sichern Sie sich jetzt Ihre kostenlose Erstberatung.`
*   **Key Sections:**
    *   **Hero:** Compelling headline ("IT, auf das Sie sich verlassen können."), value proposition, prominent CTA ("Kostenlose Beratung anfordern"), and key trust logos (Microsoft, Veeam).
    *   **Social Proof Bar:** A scrolling or static display of logos of key clients and snippets from 5-star reviews.
    *   **Services Overview:** Icon-driven cards for the four main service pillars, linking to detail pages.
    *   **"Why Us?" Snapshot:** A condensed version of the `/warum-wir` page, highlighting the 3 key differentiators (Personal, Certified, Proven).
    *   **Meet the Founder:** A brief section introducing Thomas Wimmer with a photo, linking to the full `/ueber-uns` page.
    *   **Final CTA:** A clear, final call to action to contact them.

**2. Services Overview (`/it-leistungen`)**
*   **Purpose:** To provide a clear overview of all service categories and guide users to the specific solution they need.
*   **SEO Title:** `IT-Leistungen für KMU | Wimmer EDV | St. Pölten`
*   **Meta Description:** `Umfassende IT-Services aus einer Hand: IT-Sicherheit, Cloud-Lösungen, Support und Hardware für Unternehmen in St. Pölten und Umgebung.`
*   **Key Sections:**
    *   **Intro:** Brief text explaining their philosophy of comprehensive, proactive support.
    *   **Service Pillar Grid:** Large, clickable cards for each of the four main services, each with a short, benefit-focused description.
    *   **"Not sure what you need?" CTA:** A section prompting users to contact them for a consultation.

**3. Service Detail Pages (e.g., `/it-leistungen/it-sicherheit`)**
*   **Purpose:** To act as SEO-optimized landing pages that convert visitors interested in a specific service.
*   **SEO Title:** `IT-Sicherheit für Unternehmen | Firewall & Antivirus | Wimmer EDV`
*   **Meta Description:** `Schützen Sie Ihr Unternehmen vor Cyber-Bedrohungen. Wimmer EDV bietet professionelle Firewall-, Antivirus- und Backup-Lösungen für KMU in St. Pölten.`
*   **Key Sections:**
    *   **Hero:** Service-specific headline ("Schützen Sie, was Ihr Unternehmen ausmacht").
    *   **Problem/Solution:** Sections that describe common business pains this service solves.
    *   **What's Included:** Bulleted list of specific features (e.g., Sophos Firewall, Managed Antivirus, Veeam Backups).
    *   **Relevant Certifications:** Prominently display the Sophos and Veeam logos here.
    *   **Client Testimonial:** A relevant quote from a client praising their reliability or security work.
    *   **Service-Specific CTA:** "Jetzt Sicherheits-Check anfordern".

**4. Why Wimmer EDV? (`/warum-wir`)**
*   **Purpose:** This is the core sales page, explicitly stating the company's unique value proposition.
*   **SEO Title:** `Warum Wimmer EDV? Ihr verlässlicher IT-Partner.`
*   **Meta Description:** `Persönliche Betreuung, höchste Zertifizierungen (Microsoft Gold Partner) und nachweislich perfekter Service. Erfahren Sie, was uns auszeichnet.`
*   **Key Sections:**
    *   **The Wimmer EDV Difference:** Three dedicated sections with large icons/visuals:
        1.  **Persönlich & Verantwortlich:** The owner-led advantage.
        2.  **Gold-Standard Expertise:** The story behind the certifications.
        3.  **Bewiesen & Gelobt:** A showcase of 5-star reviews and testimonials.
    *   **Comparison/Checklist:** A subtle "What to look for in an IT partner" section that reinforces their strengths.

**5. About Us (`/ueber-uns`)**
*   **Purpose:** To tell the human story behind the company, building personal trust.
*   **SEO Title:** `Über Uns | Das Team hinter Wimmer EDV`
*   **Meta Description:** `Lernen Sie Gründer Thomas Wimmer und seine Philosophie kennen. Über 10 Jahre Erfahrung für Ihren Erfolg.`
*   **Key Sections:**
    *   **Founder's Story:** A professional photo of Thomas Wimmer with a narrative written in the first or third person about his experience and vision.
    *   **Our Mission/Values:** A short, punchy section on their commitment to reliability, partnership, and clarity.
    *   **Team Section (Future-proof):** A place to add future team members.

**Internal Linking Strategy:** The homepage will link to all major pages. Service pages will cross-link to related services. The `/warum-wir` page will be linked from the header navigation and from CTAs on other pages to reinforce their value proposition. Blog posts will link to relevant service pages.
