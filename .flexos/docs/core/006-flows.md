### Key User Journeys & Conversion Flows

This document maps out the primary pathways visitors will take through the new website. Understanding these journeys allows us to optimize page content and calls-to-action (CTAs) to meet user needs and achieve business goals.

---

#### **Journey 1: The "IT Emergency"**

This user is actively seeking immediate help for a critical IT problem. They are stressed, time-sensitive, and need to find a competent provider fast.

*   **Persona:** "The Stressed Decision-Maker"
*   **Entry Point:** Google search for "IT hilfe St. Pölten" or "Computer Notdienst Firma." Lands on the **Homepage (`/`)**.
*   **Flow:**
    1.  **Homepage:** Immediately sees the headline "Zuverlässige IT-Hilfe in St. Pölten" and the phone number in the sticky header. The Microsoft Gold logo and 5-star rating provide instant credibility.
    2.  **Action (Mobile):** Taps the click-to-call phone number in the header. **Conversion complete.**
    3.  **Action (Desktop):** Scans the page for contact info, clicks the large "Kontakt" button in the navigation.
    4.  **Contact Page (`/kontakt`):** Sees the phone number displayed prominently at the top of the page and calls. **Conversion complete.**
*   **Key Optimizations:**
    *   Phone number must be the most prominent element in the header and on the contact page.
    *   Homepage hero must immediately communicate "fast, reliable help."
    *   Trust signals (certs, reviews) must be visible "above the fold."

#### **Journey 2: The "Proactive Planner"**

This user is not in a crisis but is researching options for a long-term IT partner or a specific project (e.g., migrating to Microsoft 365). They are detail-oriented and looking for proof of expertise.

*   **Persona:** "The Proactive Planner"
*   **Entry Point:** Google search for "Microsoft 365 Partner St. Pölten." Lands directly on the **Cloud Solutions Service Page (`/it-leistungen/cloud-loesungen`)**.
*   **Flow:**
    1.  **Service Page:** Reads the benefit-focused description of their Microsoft 365 services. Sees the Microsoft Gold Partner logo, which validates their expertise.
    2.  **Exploration:** Clicks on the main navigation link to the **"Why Wimmer EDV?" Page (`/warum-wir`)** to learn what makes them different.
    3.  **Validation:** On the `/warum-wir` page, they are impressed by the combination of certifications, 5-star reviews, and the personal accountability of an owner-led firm.
    4.  **Deeper Dive:** Clicks to the **Client Success Page (`/kundenerfolge`)** to read testimonials from other local businesses.
    5.  **Action:** Clicks the persistent CTA button "Kostenlose Beratung anfordern."
    6.  **Contact Page (`/kontakt`):** Fills out the contact form with details about their project. **Conversion complete.**
*   **Key Optimizations:**
    *   Service pages must be strong, SEO-optimized landing pages.
    *   A clear path from any service page to the core value proposition (`/warum-wir`) is essential.
    *   Testimonials must be easy to find and compelling.
    *   The primary CTA should be for a "consultation" or "analysis," not just "contact."

#### **Journey 3: The "Local Search & Verification"**

This user has been referred by word-of-mouth or found the business on Google Maps. They are visiting the site to verify the company's legitimacy and get a feel for their professionalism before calling.

*   **Persona:** Can be either persona.
*   **Entry Point:** Direct navigation to the **Homepage (`/`)** or found via Google Business Profile.
*   **Flow:**
    1.  **Homepage:** Gets an immediate impression of professionalism from the clean design and clear messaging.
    2.  **Human Connection:** Clicks on the **"About Us" Page (`/ueber-uns`)** to see who is behind the company.
    3.  **Trust Building:** Reading about Thomas Wimmer's 10+ years of experience builds significant trust. The professional photo helps make a personal connection.
    4.  **Confirmation:** Navigates to the **Contact Page (`/kontakt`)** to confirm the address and opening hours.
    5.  **Action:** Feels confident and calls the number listed on the site. **Conversion complete.**
*   **Key Optimizations:**
    *   The design must be impeccable to convey professionalism.
    *   The `/ueber-uns` page is critical for this journey; it must be compelling.
    *   Contact information and address must be accurate and easy to find.
