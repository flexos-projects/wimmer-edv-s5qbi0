### Description

This feature is a dedicated "About Us" page designed to humanize the brand and build a personal connection with potential clients. Instead of generic corporate text, this page will focus on the founder, Thomas Wimmer. It will feature his professional photo and a detailed biography that tells the story of his 10+ years of experience, the company's philosophy, and its commitment to clients.

### User Stories

*   **As a potential client,** I want to know who is behind the business, so I can associate the company's expertise with a real, accountable person.
*   **As a business owner who values relationships,** I want to read about the company's story and values to ensure they align with my own before I consider working with them.
*   **As a visitor,** I want to easily find this page from the main navigation to learn more about the company's background.

### Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A dedicated page is created with a user-friendly URL (e.g., `/about`).
- [ ] The page is linked in the site's main header navigation.
- [ ] A professional, high-resolution photograph of the founder, Thomas Wimmer, is prominently displayed.
- [ ] A well-written biography is present, detailing his experience and the company's philosophy.
- [ ] The content is formatted for easy readability using headings, subheadings, and paragraphs.
- [ ] The page is fully responsive and looks professional on all devices.

### Technical Notes

*   **CMS Integration:** The founder's photo and biography text must be easily updatable via the CMS.
*   **Image Optimization:** The photograph should be compressed for the web to ensure fast page load times without sacrificing quality.
*   **SEO:** The page title and meta description should be optimized for search engines (e.g., "About [Company Name] | Thomas Wimmer").
